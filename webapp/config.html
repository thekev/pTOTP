Important information about your security:
The TOTP tokens you enter here are transmitted directly to your watch. They are not sent over the network, nor stored on your phone, nor backed up to Pebble's servers. To the best of my knowledge, there is no way to recover tokens' keys once they are sent to the watch.

If you unload the app from your watch:
As the tokens are stored only on your watch, unininstalling the watch app will delete them permanently.

Please do not make this app - or any other - the sole means of accessing your accounts.
